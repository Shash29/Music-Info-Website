Jazz

What is Jazz?
An academic definition of Jazz would be:A genre of American Music that originated in New Orleans,characterized by strong, prominent meter,improvisation,distinctive note colors & performance techniques,and dotted or syncopated rhythmic patterns.

Cultural Origin:- Late 19th Century and early 20th Century,African-American communities of New Orleans,United States

Stylistic Origins:- Blues,Folk,Ragtime,Marches,Spirituals,Classical

Typically Used Instruments:-
SaxophoneKeyboardBass
DrumsGuitarsHorn
Vocals

Distinguishing Factors:- Inability to follow the song,Lots of Bass,Incorporation of instruments like Saxophone
