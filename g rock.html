Rock

What is rock?
Originated as Rock & Roll,its a genre which drew heavily on the African-American genres of Blues and Rhythm & Blues,and from country music... Musically,Rock is centered on the Electric Guitar,usually as a part of a rock group with Electric Bass,Drums and one or more Singers.

Cultural Origin:- in 1950's and 1960's,United States and UK

Stylistic Origins:- Rock & Roll,Blues,Electric Blues,Folk,Country,Rhythm & Blues

Typically Used Instruments:-
Electric GuitarBass GuitarDrums
KeyboardSaxophoneVocals

Distinguishing Factors:- Distorted Guitars,Heavy Drums
