What is Pop?
Pop is a genre of Popular music that originated in its modern form in the United States and UK. Pop music is eclectic, and often borrows elements from other styles such as urban,dance,rock,Latin and country;nonetheless,there are core elements that define Pop music.

Cultural Origin:- Mid 1950's,United States and UK

Stylistic Origins:- traditional Pop,Rock & Roll,Folk

Typically Used Instruments:-
GuitarBassPiano
DrumsCymbalElectric Organ
Electric pianoPolyphonic Tape

Distinguishing Factors:- Soft Songs with the same looped beats,Singer mostly singing throughout the song
