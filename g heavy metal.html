Heavy Metal

What is Heavy Metal?
Heavy Metal,a genre of music that includes a group of related styles that are intense,virtuosic and powerful. Driven by the aggressive sounds of the distorted Electric guitar,heavy metal is arguably the most commercially successful genre.

Cultural Origin:- Late 1960's and early 1970's,UK

Stylistic Origins:- Blues Rock,Acid Rock,Psychedelic Rock

Typically Used Instruments:-
Electric GuitarBass GuitarDrums
Vocals

Distinguishing Factors:- Heavy Sounding, hard to follow, Harsh vocals,Tons of riffs and solos
